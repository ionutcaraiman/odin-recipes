Pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Add flour and water and sugar
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Create a pizza from the dough
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Top it off with mozarella
  6. Cook in the oven 15 minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy with a glass of cold Coca-Cola
