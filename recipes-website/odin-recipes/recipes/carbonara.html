Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino
  • Guanciale
  • Eggs
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps on how to make Carbonara

  1. Start by boiling the pasta in a large pot with salt
  2. Drop the pasta in the water
  3. Cook the guanciale untill translucent
  4. Grate the pecorino and add the eggs and black pepper to it
  5. Add pasta water to pecorino and eggs mix
  6. Turn off the stove and add the pecorino mix and mix it until creamy
