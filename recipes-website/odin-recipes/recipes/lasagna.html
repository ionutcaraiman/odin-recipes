Lasagna

Lasagna, the best recipe you can get

Lasagna has its origins in Italy, it is one of the most appreciated meals over there and it's extremely delicious

Today, we are going to guide you on how to make the perfect lasagna you ever tasted, you need the following things in order to make it:

Ingredients

  • Pork mince
  • Veal mince
  • Pancetta
  • Carrots
  • Apio
  • Onions
  • Red or white wine
  • Tomato puree
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut the carrots, onion and celery
  2. Add meat
  3. Cook the meat until brown and all excess water is gone
  4. Add wine, let it simmer 5 minutes
  5. Add tomato puree
  6. Add water
  7. Cook 4-5 hours
  8. Add sauce to pasta and enjoy
